Hi there! I am a Research Engineer at [AT&T Labs](https://about.att.com/sites/labs) in Bedminster, New Jersey. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2025 from [The University of Texas at Austin](https://www.utexas.edu/), where I was a research assistant with the [Wireless Networking and Communications Group (WNCG)](https://wncg.org/) under the supervision of [Prof. Lili Qiu](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/~lili/) and [Prof. Sanjay Shakkottai](https://sites.google.com/view/sanjay-shakkottai/home). My research focuses on wireless networking and mobile computing.

Before joining UT Austin, I earned my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (Communication Theory and Systems) from [University of California San Diego (UCSD)](https://www.ucsd.edu/) in 2020, where I worked as a research assistant in the wireless networking group under the guidance of [Prof. Xinyu Zhang](http://xyzhang.ucsd.edu/). I received my B.Eng. with honors in Network Engineering from [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://en.uestc.edu.cn/) in 2018.

I have also worked with [AT&T Labs](https://about.att.com/sites/labs) as a research intern during the summers of 2022, 2023, and 2024, focusing on analytics and automation for next-generation cellular networks. During this time, I was advised by [Dr. Ajay Mahimkar](https://sites.google.com/site/ajaymahimkar/) and Dr. Zihui Ge.

You can find my CV [here](https://changhange.github.io/assets/pdf/CGe_cv.pdf).
